Privacy Policy

Lorevyn is built on a single principle: your reading life belongs to you. This policy explains exactly what that means in practice.

Effective: March 2026

Lorevyn ("we", "us", "our") operates the Lorevyn Android application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the App. Please read it carefully.

The short version: Your reading data lives on your device. We do not collect it, store it, or sell it. We do not serve ads. We do not track your behaviour. The sections below explain the details.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Lorevyn does not collect, transmit, or store on any server the following data that you create within the App:

  • Your book library, reading history, or reading progress
  • Your ratings, reviews, moods, or reading notes
  • Your reading streaks, goals, or statistics
  • Your Reading Fingerprint or persona
  • Your companion character selection or preferences
  • Any personal information you enter into the App

All of this data is stored exclusively in a private SQLite database on your device. It never leaves your device except when you explicitly choose to export it yourself.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • Book library data: titles, authors, cover images, reading status, progress, ratings, moods, and notes
  • Reading sessions: dates, pages read, session duration, and mood tags
  • App preferences: language, companion selection, notification settings, and reading goals
  • Subscription status: whether you are a free or Premium user, stored locally to provide the correct app experience

Reading notes are encrypted at rest. Your private notes are encrypted using Android's security library before being written to the database. Even if someone were to access your device's storage directly, your notes would not be readable.

3. Third-Party Services

Lorevyn makes limited, necessary calls to third-party APIs. We are transparent about every one of them.

3.1 Google Books API

When you search for a book in Lorevyn, your search query is sent to the Google Books API to retrieve book information (titles, authors, descriptions, cover images). This is a standard search request. No personally identifying information is included. Google's privacy policy applies to this API call. You can search for books without creating a Google account.

3.2 NYT Books API

The Discover tab displays New York Times bestseller lists. This involves a call to the NYT Books API that does not include any personal data — it simply retrieves public bestseller list data. The NYT's privacy policy applies to this call.

3.3 Google Play Billing

If you purchase a Lorevyn Premium subscription, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. Lorevyn does not receive or store your payment information. Google Play's privacy policy governs payment data. We receive only a confirmation that a valid subscription exists, which is used to unlock Premium features.

3.4 No Analytics or Crash Reporting

Lorevyn does not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Crashlytics, Sentry, or any other analytics or crash-reporting service. We do not track how you use the App. We do not receive crash reports unless you choose to send them manually through the Android system.

4. Advertising

Lorevyn contains no advertising of any kind. We do not work with advertising networks. We do not use your data to serve targeted ads. We do not sell your data to advertisers. This will never change — our business model is subscription revenue, not advertising.

5. Data Import

Lorevyn allows you to import your reading history from Goodreads and StoryGraph by selecting a file from your device. This file is processed entirely on your device. It is never uploaded to any server. We do not transmit, store, or access your imported data remotely.

6. Data Export

You can export your Lorevyn library at any time from the Settings menu. The exported file is created on your device and shared using Android's standard file-sharing system. We do not receive a copy of your export. Your data belongs to you, and export is always free regardless of subscription status.

7. Children's Privacy

Lorevyn is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us at the address below.

8. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using a device lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face recognition). Reading notes are encrypted at rest as an additional layer of protection. We do not control and are not responsible for the security of your device itself.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Effective" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide notice within the App. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about how Lorevyn handles your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@lorevyn.app
  • Website: lorevyn.app

Lorevyn is an independent app. We are not affiliated with Goodreads, StoryGraph, Amazon, Google, or the New York Times beyond the API integrations described above.
