Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Lorevyn is built on a simple principle: your reading life belongs to you. All data you enter stays on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers. Nothing is shared with third parties. No ads. Ever.

Who We Are

Lorevyn is developed and published by Richey AI Labs. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at privacy@lorevyn.app.

What Data We Collect

Lorevyn does not collect personal data. The app does not require an account, does not connect to our servers, and does not transmit any information about you or your reading habits to us or any third party.

All data you enter into Lorevyn, including your book library, reading sessions, notes, ratings, and goals, is stored locally on your device using an encrypted on-device database. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it yourself using the app's export feature.

Camera Permission

Lorevyn requests access to your device camera solely to scan ISBN barcodes when adding books to your library. The camera is used only when you actively tap the scan button. No photos or images are stored, transmitted, or processed outside of your device. The camera feed is used only for barcode detection and is discarded immediately after a scan is completed or cancelled.

Data You Export

Lorevyn allows you to export your library as a JSON or CSV file. This export is initiated entirely by you and is saved directly to your device or shared using your device's built-in share sheet. Richey AI Labs does not receive or have access to any exported files.

Third-Party Services

Lorevyn uses the following external services only for book discovery and metadata lookup. These services are contacted only when you search for or scan a book.

Google Books API, used to retrieve book metadata such as title, author, cover image, and page count. Google's privacy policy applies to these requests and can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

OpenLibrary API, used as a supplementary source for book metadata. The Internet Archive's privacy policy applies and can be found at archive.org/about/terms.php.

New York Times Books API, used to display current bestseller lists in the Discover tab. The New York Times privacy policy applies and can be found at nytimes.com privacy policy.

Lorevyn does not use advertising networks, analytics services, crash reporting services, or any other third-party data collection tools.

Google Play Billing

Lorevyn offers an optional premium subscription processed entirely through Google Play. Richey AI Labs does not collect or store your payment information. All billing is handled by Google and is subject to Google Play's terms of service and privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Lorevyn is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because Lorevyn collects no data at all, there is no data relating to any user, including minors.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Because Lorevyn is a private, offline-first app and we have no way to contact users directly, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

Questions about this policy are welcome at privacy@lorevyn.app.
