Reading Fingerprint

Discover the reader behind the books

Your Reading Fingerprint is Lorevyn's most distinctive feature. Based on your actual reading data — genres, moods, pace, and patterns — it generates a unique persona that describes how you read.

  • Calculated from your real reading history, not a quiz
  • Updates automatically as you read more
  • Shareable as a beautiful card
  • Yours to keep — free, not behind a paywall
Reading Streaks

Build the habit. Track the journey.

Reading streaks and a beautiful heatmap show your reading consistency at a glance. Every session logged counts — even 10 pages before bed.

  • Daily streak tracking with milestone badges
  • Visual heatmap of your reading activity
  • Streak repair tokens so one missed day doesn't break everything
  • Gentle reminders that feel encouraging, not nagging
